Tea

Retail packs, Gift packs and Bulk form are all available at
Eco Leaf Plantations. The best tea the island has to offer in

Rubber

Latex grade no. 1X, Latex grade no. 1 and Sole crepe are some
varieties of rubber manufactured through EPP. The rubber produced
are of such high quality that our sole crepe are used in "Clark shoes".
EcoLeafPlantation rubber is some of the finest rubber produced in the country.

Palm Oil

Palm trees are grown through three main estates of EcoLeaf Plantation.
The collected crops are processed into crude palm oil through our joint
venture with AEN Palm oil processing in the factory at
Deniyaya. our palm oil products are supplied to customers who refine
and use them on their end products. Our main exports are directed to
Indian partners and already gaining a strong brand reputation for the
quality and consistency of supply. The company aims tomake strategic
investments in this regard as well as building a strong revenue
portfolio through palm oil processing.

Coconut

Coconuts are derived from three estates in EcoLeaf Plantations.
These crops are mainly used to enrich the local market and are
sold through local sellers.

Cinnamon

Ceylon Cinnamon is one of Sri Lanka's endemic produces.
EcoLeaf Plantations is a proud home to some of the best
quality cinnamon, available in a range of grades such as
C5, M5, Alba and even powder. EPP cinnamon will spice up your life.

Blackberry

Blackberries are medium-sized berries with a tart flavour,
favored around the world for its taste and numerous health benefits.
EcoLeaf Plantations brings you the country's first opportunity
to taste fresh blackberries, grown on Sri Lankan soil.

Raspberry

Sri Lanka's first, fresh grown raspberries available through
EcoLeaf Plantations PLC. A red, small and uniquely shaped berry
with an even more unique taste high in vitamin C and low in calories.
An essential superfood.

Bee Honey

The market is filled with doctored honey and it is hard to
differentiate between them. Our company gives you the promise
of genuine bee honey and all its benefits. Try a bottle of liquid gold.

Jam

As an island country, EcoLeaf Plantations has access to many
tropical fruits such as Soursop, Passionfruit and Pineapple.
These products are available in fresh form, pulp form and in
specialty jams, smoothies, etc.
